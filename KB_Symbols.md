|   !   | Bang / Exclamation mark
|   @   | At / At the rate
|   #   | Hash / Pound / Octothorpe
|   $   | Dollar
|   %   | Percent
|   ^   | Caret
|   &   | And / Ampersand
|   *   | Asterisk / Star / Times
|   -   | Dash / Hyphen / Minus
|   +   | Plus
|   =   | Equals
|   _   | Underscore
|  ( )  | Parenthesis / Round brackets
|  { }  | Braces / Curly brackets
|  [ ]  | Brackets / Square brackets
|  < >  | Tag / Pointy brackets
|   >   | Greater than
|   <   | Less than / Angle
|   '   | Single qoute
|   "   | Double qoute
|   `   | Tick / Back tick
|   ~   | Tilde
|   :   | Colon
|   ;   | Semicolon
|   .   | Period / Full Stop
|   ,   | Comma
|   /   | Slash / Forward slash
|   \   | Backslash
|   \|  | Pipe / Or
